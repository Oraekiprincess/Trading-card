Sugar

Sugar is a lovely Burmese Cat!

  • Scientific Name: Felis Catus
  • Average Length: 15-18 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 18 years
  • Habitat: They're best kept as indoor cats.

Sugar has spots covering her body, with the loveliest eyes. Sugar is loving, playful, and highly social.

She's very homely and highly inquisitive. She loves cuddles and family time. she'll be a great pet for first time owners!
